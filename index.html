Kate Hoffmire

My name is Kate Hoffmire and I am a first year MSI student focusing on UX. I returned to school after a ten year career working in market research and consumer insights for a handful of SaaS companies. My undergraduate is from BYU and is in Middle Eastern History and I minored in communications which drove my first internships and jobs in marketing and market research. I still work as a research freelancer for the last company I worked for as it is a great school job to compliment a graduate schedule.

In my career I have worked as a consumer insights researcher driven to help companies learn from quantitative and qualitative data to improve their customer experience. At Brandwatch, I lead a team of researchers managing the design of research methodology, data analysis, insights storytelling, and client consultation on how to use consumer intelligence to make strategic decisions.
